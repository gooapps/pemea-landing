CHALLENGES

Choosing the right partner can make the difference between success and failure which is why we encourage companies not to go with the first option but always keep in mind the following pitfalls when choosing your local partner.

Language and business culture barriers

Regulation and reimbursement challenges

Complexity of distribution

1.Language and business culture barriers

Navigating cross-cultural communication, etiquettes and hierarchy can be a big challenge when sitting on the other side of the globe. The most effective distribution partners for your business might lack in English or general knowledge of your business culture.

In PEMEA Consulting we are experienced selecting potential distributors, evaluating a full range of possible partners, understanding or sharing their culture, analyzing how they operate, sales and marketing capabilities, financial stability, knowledge of the market, customer relationships and more.

2.Regulation and reimbursement challenges

The regulatory structure in Middle Eastern & Latin American countries is neither uniform nor consistent and gaining success in new markets can be tough if your products are late to market or come without appropriate reimbursement.

In PEMEA Consulting we have the experience to qualify potential partners and make sure your products find the fastest way to market and profitability.

3.Complexity of distribution

In the Middle East most medical products are being sold through big tenders requiring immense promotional work beforehand. In South America medical product distribution is still evolving and many distributors do not offer effective reach throughout the whole country.

In PEMEA Consultingwe understand the strengths and weaknesses of each potential partner and how they fit into the distribution system, these are critical considerations in making the best long-term partner choices.
