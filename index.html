CHALLENGES

Choosing the right partner can make the difference between success and failure which is why we encourage companies not to go with the first option but always keep in mind the following pitfalls when choosing your local partner.

Language and business culture barriers

Navigating cross-cultural communication, etiquettes and hierarchy can be a big challenge when sitting on the other side of the globe. The most effective distribution partners for your business might lack in English or general knowledge of your business culture. Therefor the selection of a potential distributor should be done by someone on the ground who can actually evaluate the full range of a possible partner, analyzing how they operate, their sales and marketing capabilities, financial stability, knowledge of your market, customer relationships and more.

Complexity of distribution

In the Middle East most medical products are being sold through big tenders requiring an immense promotional work beforehand. In South America medical product distribution is still evolving and many distributors do not offer effective reach throughout the whole country. Understanding the strengths and weaknesses of each potential partner and how they fit into the distribution system are critical considerations in making the best long-term partner choices.

Regulation & reimbursement challenges

The regulatory structure in the Middle East & Latin American countries is neither uniform nor consistent and gaining success in new markets can be tough if your products are late to market or come without appropriate reimbursement. In PEMEA Consulting we have the experience to qualify potential partners and make sure your products find the fastest way to market and profitability.
